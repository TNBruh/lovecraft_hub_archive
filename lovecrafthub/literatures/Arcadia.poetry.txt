By Head Balledup<br><br>

O give me the life of the village,<br>
&emsp;Uninhibited, free, and sweet;<br>
The place where the arts all flourish,<br/>
&emsp;Grove Court and Christopher Street.<br/><br/>

I am sick of the old conventions,<br/>
&emsp;And critics who will not praise,<br/>
So sing ho for the open spaces,<br/>
&emsp;And aesthetes with kindly ways.<br/><br/>

Here every bard is a genius,<br/>
&emsp;And artists are Raphaels,<br/>
And above the roofs of Patchin Place<br/>
&emsp;The Muse of Talent dwells.
