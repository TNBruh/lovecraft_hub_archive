In the midnight heavens burning<br>
&emsp;Thro’ ethereal deeps afar,<br>
Once I watch’d with restless yearning<br>
&emsp;An alluring, aureate star;<br>
Ev’ry eye aloft returning,<br/>
&emsp;Gleaming nigh the Arctic car.<br/><br/>

Mystic waves of beauty blended<br/>
&emsp;With the gorgeous golden rays;<br/>
Phantasies of bliss descended<br/>
&emsp;In a myrrh’d Elysian haze;<br/>
And in lyre-born chords extended<br/>
&emsp;Harmonies of Lydian lays.<br/><br/>

There (thought I) lies scenes of pleasure,<br/>
&emsp;Where the free and blessed dwell,<br/>
And each moment bears a treasure<br/>
&emsp;Freighted with a lotus-spell,<br/>
And there floats a liquid measure<br/>
&emsp;From the lute of Israfel.<br/><br/>

There (I told myself) were shining<br/>
&emsp;Worlds of happiness unknown,<br/>
Peace and Innocence entwining<br/>
&emsp;By the Crowned Virtue’s throne;<br/>
Men of light, their thoughts refining<br/>
&emsp;Purer, fairer, than our own.<br/><br/>

Thus I mus’d, when o’er the vision<br/>
&emsp;Crept a red delirious change;<br/>
Hope dissolving to derision,<br/>
&emsp;Beauty to distortion strange;<br/>
Hymnic chords in weird collision,<br/>
&emsp;Spectral sights in endless range.<br/><br/>

Crimson burn’d the star of sadness<br/>
&emsp;As behind the beams I peer’d;<br/>
All was woe that seem’d but gladness<br/>
&emsp;Ere my gaze with truth was sear’d;<br/>
Cacodaemons, mir’d with madness,<br/>
&emsp;Thro’ the fever’d flick’ring leer’d.<br/><br/>

Now I know the fiendish fable<br/>
&emsp;That the golden glitter bore;<br/>
Now I shun the spangled sable<br/>
&emsp;That I watch’d and lov’d before;<br/>
But the horror, set and stable,<br/>
&emsp;Haunts my soul for evermore.
