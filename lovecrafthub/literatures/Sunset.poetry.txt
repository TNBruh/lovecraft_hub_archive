The cloudless day is richer at its close;<br>
&emsp;A golden glory settles on the lea;<br>
Soft, stealing shadows hint of cool repose<br>
&emsp;To mellowing landscape, and to calming sea.<br><br/>

And in that nobler, gentler, lovelier light,<br/>
&emsp;The soul to sweeter, loftier bliss inclines;<br/>
Freed form the noonday glare, the favour’d sight<br/>
&emsp;Increasing grace in earth and sky divines.<br/><br/>

But ere the purest radiance crowns the green,<br/>
&emsp;Or fairest lustre fills th’ expectant grove,<br/>
The twilight thickens, and the fleeting scene<br/>
&emsp;Leaves but a hallow’d memory of love!
